Kobe 8

The Nike Kobe 8, released in 2012, is celebrated for its ultra-lightweight design and exceptional court feel, marking one of the lightest basketball shoes Nike has ever produced. Featuring an engineered mesh upper for breathability and flexibility, it introduced a full-length Lunarlon midsole for soft, responsive cushioning.
