Kobe 10

The Nike Kobe 10, released in 2015, continued the evolution of the Kobe line with a focus on combining performance innovation and sleek design. It featured a hybrid cushioning setup with Zoom Air in the heel and Lunarlon foam in the forefoot, delivering a balance of impact protection and responsiveness.
