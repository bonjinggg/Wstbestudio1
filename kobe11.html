Kobe 11

The Nike Kobe 11, released in 2016, was the final signature shoe Kobe Bryant wore during his NBA career and served as a fitting capstone to his innovative line. It featured a low-cut design with a Flyknit upper reinforced by thermoplastic yarns, offering lightweight support, flexibility, and durability.
