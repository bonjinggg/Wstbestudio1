Kobe 1"

The Nike Zoom Kobe 1, released in 2006, was Kobe Bryant’s first signature shoe with Nike and set the tone for his legendary sneaker line. Designed for performance and versatility, it features a high-top leather upper for support, responsive Zoom Air cushioning in the heel and forefoot, and a carbon fiber shank for stability during quick movements. Its bold design reflects Kobe’s intense playing style, with classic colorways tied to his Lakers legacy. The Kobe 1 remains a significant piece of sneaker history, blending innovation with the mindset of one of basketball’s fiercest competitors.
