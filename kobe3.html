Kobe 3"

The Nike Zoom Kobe 3, released in 2008, stands out with its unique diamond-pattern mesh upper, offering lightweight support and breathability. Designed for speed and agility, it features Zoom Air cushioning and a low-profile feel, allowing Kobe Bryant to move freely and explosively on the court.
